Freelancer Portfolio Website Starter Kit
---------------------------------------

How to Launch Your Portfolio Website in 30 Minutes

1. Unzip the Kit
   - Download and extract all files to a folder.

2. Open Homepage
   - Open index.html in your browser to preview.

3. Customize Your Website
   - Edit text, images, and projects in index.html, projects.html, and contact.html.
   - Edit colors/fonts in assets/css/style.css if desired.

4. Preview
   - Open files in browser to see your changes.

5. Go Live (Optional)
   - Upload files to GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel.

Tips:
- Keep your homepage professional.
- Only showcase your best projects.
- Add clear CTA in contact page.
